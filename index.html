About

Bridging AI and Aerial Robotics

As an AI engineer deeply embedded in the drone industry, I focus on designing and deploying intelligent systems that enable unmanned aerial vehicles to perceive, reason, and act autonomously in complex environments.

My work spans the full machine learning lifecycle: from data collection and annotation pipeline design, through model architecture research and distributed training, to on-device optimization and real-time inference on edge hardware such as NVIDIA Jetson and Qualcomm platforms.

I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of what autonomous aerial systems can achieve -- from real-time object detection and semantic segmentation for obstacle avoidance, to multi-sensor fusion and path planning algorithms that operate reliably in GPS-denied environments.
